Why Choose KUPPY?

100% Biodegradable

Our bottles are made from plant-based materials that naturally break down without harming the environment.

Safe & Tamper-Proof

The top 30% acts as a tear-to-open safety seal. Customers tear the ad strip to open the bottle — the main label remains intact.

Partner-friendly Ads

Sell the top band to brands — a high-impact, low-cost ad placement that reaches consumers at the moment of use.

How It Works

Step 1 — Production

We print a full-wrap label with a perforated top strip (30% height). The bottom 70% carries the permanent KUPPY branding.

Step 2 — Distribution

Bottles reach retail, events and corporate partners with ads already printed on the tamper band.

Step 3 — Engagement

Customers tear the ad band to open the bottle — high brand recall at the moment of consumption.

About KUPPY

KUPPY began with a simple idea: everyday products should not cost the earth. We create bottles that biodegrade naturally and give brands a new way to reach customers responsibly.

FAQ

Is the bottle fully biodegradable?

Yes. Materials used are plant-based and designed to break down correctly under composting conditions.

Can I customize design for my brand?

Yes — we offer special runs and custom printing for large orders.

How does the ad band work?

The top 30% is printed as an ad band with a perforation. Consumers tear it off to open the bottle. The main KUPPY label remains on the bottle.
